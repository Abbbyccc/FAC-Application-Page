A little about me

I came to the UK from Taiwan in 2013 and ever since then I have been working as a project manager mainly focused on the education training sector between the UK and China. And due to my interests, I also helped my companies to build and maintain websites which build up my interest in web development even more. I always have this thought to change my career to the tech industry but never be brave enough to do so, until the pandemic came which changed how the world’s running and showed how important technology is for the future. This is really a wake-up call to me, I finally made up my mind to change my career to become a web developer. I started to take HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from multiple platforms at end of 2021 and I am fully ready to take another step with Founders & Coders to start my new journey!

Why Founders & Coders ?

I have been self-learning basic coding skills but I find it struggle sometimes until my friend recommended FAC to me. When I was invited to join the Discord channel, I immediately love the friendly and supportive environment it provides. I feel more confident being surrounded by same-minded people who have the same interests, and goals which also reassured me that I am not alone and that there are many people who are choosing the same path as me. Most importantly, FAC provides peep to peer learning, project works, and apprenticeship opportunities all to train individuals to become more employable and provide a career path for students which is rare compared to normal pricey Bootcamps. I also like the learning style, I love to share my idea, help and discuss with each other and always be respectful. I believe FAC will provide the experience that I will never get by self-learning and be the best choice I made for my journey.
